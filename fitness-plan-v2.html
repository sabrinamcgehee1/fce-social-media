Daily Walk — Every Single Day (Non-Negotiable)

Your Grow with Jo walk is your foundation. 20–30 min with the pram fully counts. On strength days, do it before or after — whatever works with the twins.

⚠️

Diastasis Recti (5cm) — Always Keep in Mind

  • Crunches, sit-ups, or any spinal flexion
  • Full planks on hands (until doming stops)
  • Double leg lowers · The Pilates "hundred"
  • Dead bugs — safe & healing ✓
  • Glute bridges — excellent ✓
  • Squats & lunges — great ✓
  • Forearm planks (short holds, no doming) ✓
  • Deep core breathing before every session ✓

Progressive Overload System

Make it harder every 2 weeks — this is what drives results.

Phase 1 — Now
Weeks 1–2
Learn the videos. 3 rounds. Focus on form. No load.
Phase 2
Weeks 3–4
+2 reps per exercise OR slow tempo (3 sec down). Harder Pilates videos.
Phase 3
Weeks 5–6
4 rounds. Backpack load for squats/bridges if DR ok. Introduce kettlebell.
Phase 4
Weeks 7–8
Heavier load, harder variations, full kettlebell sessions. You'll feel transformed.

          Nutrition — Critical on Mounjaro

          Eat well, prioritise protein, stay hydrated. No tracking needed — just these principles.

          🚨

          Mounjaro Muscle Loss Risk — This Is Serious

          Because Mounjaro reduces appetite significantly, many people eat too little total food — and too little protein. Combined with a calorie deficit, your body can start burning muscle for fuel. This makes the "skinny fat" outcome more likely and slows your metabolism long-term. The fix: protein first, every single meal.

          🥩
          Protein — Priority #1

          Aim for 100–120g per day. Eat it first at every meal. Greek yogurt, eggs, chicken, fish, cottage cheese, protein shake.

          💧
          Hydration — Priority #2

          Mounjaro + exercise = high dehydration risk. Aim for 2.5–3L water daily. Carry a large bottle everywhere.

          🥗
          Vegetables — Fill the Plate

          After protein, fill with vegetables. Fibre, micronutrients, fullness without excessive calories.

          🫐
          Don't Skip Meals

          Mounjaro reduces hunger — this is NOT a sign to skip meals. Eat 3 structured meals even if small.

          💊
          Multivitamin — Daily

          Eating less means fewer micronutrients. Take with your biggest meal. Add vitamin D3 and magnesium.

          🍳
          Easy High-Protein Meals

          Greek yogurt + berries · Eggs + avocado · Cottage cheese + fruit · Chicken + veg · Protein shake + banana.

          Mounjaro Progression Guide

          Always follow your prescriber's guidance.

          Starting
          2.5mg
          Weeks 1–4. Adjustment phase. Nausea common. Eat small, bland, protein-first.
          Building
          5mg
          Weeks 5–8. Most people feel the real effect here. Protein discipline is critical.
          Progressing
          7.5mg
          If advised by prescriber. Stronger appetite suppression. Hit protein targets.
          Advanced
          10mg+
          Prescriber guidance only. Your workout habits protect your muscle at this stage.
