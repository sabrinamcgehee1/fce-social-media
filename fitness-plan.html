Your Home Strength Circuit

Reference guide for all exercises. Click ✏️ Edit on any exercise to customise it. These match the videos on the Follow-Along page.

⏱️

Before Every Strength Session — 5 min DR Core Warm-Up

Deep core breathing (90/90 position): inhale to expand belly sideways, exhale to gently draw navel in. 10 breaths. Activates deep core, protects your DR. Never skip.

Monday & Friday — Lower Body + Core
3 rounds · 45 sec rest between rounds · ~25 min total
  • 1
    Bodyweight Squats — 12 repsFeet shoulder-width. Sit back into heels. Chest tall. Progress: slow tempo or backpack (weeks 5+)
  • 2
    Glute Bridges — 15 repsLie on back, feet flat. Drive hips up, squeeze glutes. 2-sec hold. DR-safe and excellent.
  • 3
    Reverse Lunges — 10 reps each legStep back (easier on knees). Front knee over ankle. Hold wall if needed.
  • 4
    Dead Bugs — 8 reps each sideExtend opposite arm + leg. Lower back stays pressed to floor. Exhale as you extend.
  • 5
    Heel Slides — 12 reps each legSlide heel out and back along floor. Breathe out as you slide. Deep core activation.
🔁 Rest 45 sec · Repeat × 3 rounds
Tuesday — Upper Body + Core
3 rounds · 45 sec rest between rounds · ~25 min total
  • 1
    Wall Push-Ups — 12 repsHands on wall, step feet back. Progress: incline push-ups (weeks 3–4), knee push-ups (weeks 5+)
  • 2
    Tricep Dips — 10 repsHands on chair behind you. Elbows go back, not out. Skip if shoulder discomfort.
  • 3
    Superman Holds — 10 reps (3-sec hold)Lie face down. Lift opposite arm and leg. Builds back — vital for twin carrying posture.
  • 4
    Dead Bugs — 8 reps each sideCore work every session for DR healing.
  • 5
    Shoulder Taps — 10 reps (forearm plank)Only if zero doming. If doming — swap to bird-dogs on all fours.
🔁 Rest 45 sec · Repeat × 3 rounds
Thursday — Full Body
3 rounds · 45 sec rest between rounds · ~28 min total
  • 1
    Bodyweight Squats — 12 repsSame as Monday. Add backpack from week 5 if DR allows.
  • 2
    Glute Bridges — 15 reps2-sec hold at top. Add pillow resistance from week 5.
  • 3
    Reverse Lunges — 10 reps each legHold wall for balance if needed.
  • 4
    Wall Push-Ups — 12 repsProgress to incline then knee push-ups as you get stronger.
  • 5
    Dead Bugs — 8 reps each sideCore work every session — non-negotiable for DR.
  • 6
    Heel Slides — 12 reps each legDeep core activation before the next round.
🔁 Rest 45 sec · Repeat × 3 rounds
💡

When to Progress Each Exercise

  • Exercise feels easy 2 sessions in a row → add 2 reps next session
  • Still easy? → slow the tempo (3 sec down, 1 sec up)
  • Still easy? → increase rounds from 3 to 4
  • DR healing well? → add backpack load
  • Quality always beats quantity

Weight Recommendation

You mentioned buying a 10kg. That's fine for squats and deadlifts, but most beginners find 8kg better for swings, cleans, and presses. If budget allows, having both an 8kg and 10kg is ideal. Use 8kg for technique work, 10kg once movements feel solid. If you only have 10kg — just reduce reps and go slower on technique moves.

Nutrition — Critical on Mounjaro

Eat well, prioritise protein, stay hydrated. No tracking needed — just these principles.

🚨

Mounjaro Muscle Loss Risk — This Is Serious

Because Mounjaro reduces appetite significantly, many people eat too little total food — and too little protein. Combined with a calorie deficit, your body can start burning muscle for fuel. This makes the "skinny fat" outcome more likely and slows your metabolism long-term. The fix: protein first, every single meal.

🥩
Protein — Priority #1

Aim for 100–120g per day. Eat it first at every meal. Greek yogurt, eggs, chicken, fish, cottage cheese, protein shake.

💧
Hydration — Priority #2

Mounjaro + exercise = high dehydration risk. Aim for 2.5–3L water daily. Carry a large bottle everywhere.

🥗
Vegetables — Fill the Plate

After protein, fill with vegetables. Fibre, micronutrients, fullness without excessive calories.

🫐
Don't Skip Meals

Mounjaro reduces hunger — this is NOT a sign to skip meals. Eat 3 structured meals even if small.

💊
Multivitamin — Daily

Eating less means fewer micronutrients. Take with your biggest meal. Add vitamin D3 and magnesium.

🍳
Easy High-Protein Meals

Greek yogurt + berries · Eggs + avocado · Cottage cheese + fruit · Chicken + veg · Protein shake + banana.
