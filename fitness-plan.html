Why This Plan Is Different From Your Old One

Your original plan was great for starting out — but it leaned too heavily on Pilates and light cardio. On Mounjaro, your body is at high risk of losing muscle instead of fat if you don't give it enough strength stimulus. This upgraded plan keeps your 30-min limit and home setup, but adds 3 strength days that will protect your muscle, accelerate fat loss, and get you to your 15kg goal with tone.

Progressive Overload System

This is the game changer. Your body adapts — you must keep making it harder every 2 weeks.

Phase 1 — Now
Weeks 1–2
Learn the circuit. 3 rounds. Focus on form. 10–12 reps per exercise. No load.
Phase 2
Weeks 3–4
+2 reps per exercise OR slow tempo (3 sec down). Still 3 rounds. Small wins.
Phase 3
Weeks 5–6
4 rounds. Backpack for squats/bridges if DR ok. Harder push-up variation.

Weeks 7–8
Heavier load, longer holds, more challenging variations. You will feel like a different person.

Your Home Strength Circuit

No equipment needed. DR-safe. Done in 25–30 minutes. Do this 3x per week.

⏱️

Before Every Strength Session — 5 min Core Warm-Up

Deep core breathing (90/90 position): inhale to expand belly sideways, exhale to gently draw navel in. 10 breaths. This activates your deep core and protects your DR before every session. Never skip this.

Monday & Friday — Lower Body + Core Circuit
3 rounds · 45 sec rest between rounds · ~20 min total
  • 1
    Bodyweight Squats — 12 reps Feet shoulder-width. Sit back into heels. Keep chest tall. Progress: slow tempo or loaded backpack (weeks 5+)
  • 2
    Glute Bridges — 15 reps Lie on back, feet flat. Drive hips up, squeeze glutes at top. 2-sec hold. DR-safe and excellent for postpartum. Progress: pillow on hips for resistance (weeks 5+)
  • 3
    Reverse Lunges — 10 reps each leg Step back, not forward (easier on knees). Keep front knee over ankle. Hold wall if balance is off — that's completely fine with a 9-month-old body.
  • 4
    Dead Bugs — 8 reps each side Lie on back. Extend opposite arm + leg slowly. Lower back stays pressed to floor. Exhale as you extend. This is your core healer — do it with full attention.
  • 5
    Heel Slides — 12 reps each leg Lie on back. Slide heel out and back along the floor. Breathe out as you slide out. Deep core activation. Great for DR healing.
🔁 Rest 45 sec, then repeat x3 total
↗ Watch: Dead bugs & heel slides tutorial
Wednesday — Upper Body + Core Circuit
3 rounds · 45 sec rest between rounds · ~20 min total
  • 1
    Wall Push-Ups — 12 reps Hands on wall, step feet back. Lower chest to wall, push back. Progress: incline push-ups on low surface (weeks 3–4), knee push-ups (weeks 5+)
  • 2
    Tricep Dips — 10 reps Hands on chair behind you, feet flat on floor. Lower and push back up. Elbows go back, not out. Skip if shoulder discomfort.
  • 3
    Superman Holds — 10 reps (3-sec hold) Lie face down. Lift opposite arm and leg simultaneously. Hold 3 sec. This builds your back, which is critical for posture after carrying twins.
  • 4
    Dead Bugs — 8 reps each side Same as lower body day. Core work every session is non-negotiable for your DR healing.
  • 5
    Shoulder Taps — 10 reps (forearm plank position) Forearm plank, tap each shoulder alternately. Only if zero doming. If doming occurs — stop and do bird-dogs on all fours instead.
🔁 Rest 45 sec, then repeat x3 total
↗ Watch: MadFit upper body beginner
💡

When to Progress Each Exercise

  • Exercise feels easy for 2 sessions in a row → add 2 reps next session
  • Still easy? → slow the tempo (3 sec down, pause, 1 sec up)
  • Still easy? → increase rounds from 3 to 4
  • DR healing well? → add backpack load for squats and bridges
  • Never rush progression — quality beats quantity always

Nutrition — Critical on Mounjaro

The biggest mistake on Mounjaro is eating too little protein. It leads to muscle loss, worse fatigue, and a slower metabolism. Here's the priority order.

🚨

Mounjaro Muscle Loss Risk — This Is Serious

Because Mounjaro reduces appetite significantly, many people eat too little total food — and too little protein. Combined with a calorie deficit, your body can start burning muscle for fuel. This makes the "skinny fat" outcome more likely and slows your metabolism long-term. The fix: protein first, every single meal, before anything else on your plate.

🥩
Protein — Priority #1

Aim for 100–120g protein per day. Eat it first at every meal. Greek yogurt, eggs, chicken, fish, cottage cheese, protein shake. Even if you're not hungry — prioritise protein over everything else.

💧
Hydration — Priority #2

Mounjaro + breastfeeding (if applicable) + exercise = high dehydration risk. Aim for 2.5–3L water daily. Carry a large bottle everywhere. Electrolytes are helpful too.

🥗
Vegetables — Fill the Plate

After protein, fill the rest of your plate with vegetables. They provide fibre, micronutrients, and keep you feeling full without calories. Don't restrict them.

🫐
Don't Skip Meals

Mounjaro will make you less hungry — this is NOT a sign to skip meals. Eat 3 structured meals even if small. Skipping accelerates muscle loss and worsens fatigue.

💊
Multivitamin — Daily

Eating less means fewer micronutrients. A good multivitamin is non-negotiable. Take it with your biggest meal to reduce nausea. Consider adding vitamin D3 and magnesium.

🍳
High-Protein Easy Meals

Greek yogurt + berries. Scrambled eggs + avocado. Cottage cheese + fruit. Protein shake + banana. Chicken + veg stir fry. These are fast, high protein, and twin-friendly.

Mounjaro Progression Guide

Most results accelerate from 5mg upward. Always follow your prescriber's guidance.

Starting
2.5mg
Weeks 1–4. Adjustment phase. Nausea common. Eat small, bland, protein-first meals.
Building
5mg
Weeks 5–8. Most people feel the real effect here. Appetite drops significantly — protein discipline is critical.
Progressing
7.5mg
If advised by prescriber. Stronger appetite suppression. Even more critical to hit protein targets.
Advanced
10mg+
With prescriber guidance only. At this stage, your workout and protein habits protect your muscle.
